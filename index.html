Hosts

The J. Barkev M. Kassarjian Charitable Trust

Our mission is to strengthen the Armenian entrepreneurial ecosystem by investing in founders and educators who shape enduring economic and human impact.

What We Believe

Armenia's greatest entrepreneurial advantage isn't found in solo genius— it's cultivated through ensemble leadership. By supporting case method teaching (based on Armenian entrepreneurs and companies), human-centered leadership development, and holistic learning experiences in Armenia's academic and entrepreneurial communities, we're building a generation of founders who lead as voracious learners, develop high-performing teams, and create companies with lasting impact.

Established in honor of J. Barkev M. Kassarjian - educator, business advisor, and champion of original thinking.

“BUILD SOMETHING THAT WOULD NOT EXIST IF YOU DIDNT BUILD IT."

- J.B. Kassarjian

In life, JB Kassarjian developed entrepreneurs and business leaders from Boston to Shanghai. He saw the role of the entrepreneur as the leader of an ensemble, all improvising together to create something beautiful.

Performance of a Lifetime

We bring to the Armenian entrepreneurial ecosystem a 25-year track record of growing leaders inside Google, Pfizer, Infosys, American Express, and hundreds of other demanding organizations.

We don't lecture. We don't do theory. We engage accomplished entrepreneurs in working through their real challenges — together — and building the leadership needed to drive what’s next.

The capabilities at the heart of this Summit emerged directly from conversations with Armenian VCs and founders who told us what separates the entrepreneurs who break through from those who plateau. We listened. This Summit is our response.
